How It Works

Prepare Photos

Place your photos in sandwich bags or rubber banded together by album or event. Provide a name and date if known. Photos will be organized into folders and include dates in the files.

Drop off Photos

No shipping necessary! Drop off your photos near Freeman Park off (Route 3 and Freeman Road).

Scan

You photos are scanned, processed and loaded into an online photo vault. If handwriting is detected on the back, both the front and back is scanned.

Share Instantly

Receive all of your files with a digital download link and photo vault access via email as soon as processing is complete. Your original photos can be picked up at any time.
